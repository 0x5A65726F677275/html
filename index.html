Philosophy

"Pianist should always try to become a good singer, since in reality, we are using keys just as vocalist use their vocal cords to resonate sound."

His Work

His composition blends a wide range of genres—pop, rock, jazz, musical, praise song, and impressionistic elements—while keeping a strong sense of tonality. Rather than following the popular atonal approach of 20th-century classical music, he focuses on creating music that can reach broader audiences without losing its distinctive aura.
